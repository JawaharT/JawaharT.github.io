Summer 2020

I made an automated program that outlines chondrocytes and corresponding nuclei on immunofluorecent images that measure YAP (protein) using multiple packages including but not limited to computer vision (CV2) in Python for my undergraduate dissertation of BEng Biomedical Engineering. The dissertation involved comparing the manual quantfication method and the reliability of the results and submitted before the summer started. Expanded Computational Biology knowledge with Biopython and integrated its functionality with a GUI to convert a given DNA sequence into its protein code. Also other Python packages such as Scikit-Bio and Biotite.

I made a GUI rock, paper, scissors game that keeps a rolling record of the results. Also started revising important mathematical concepts such as eignavalues and eiganvectors for upcoming enrolment in MSc Computer Science. A lot more attention regarding biomedical technology has become apparent due to the global pandemic, making biological and programming skills more valuable in this day and age.
